Learning and growing through the Nobel Internship program

Nobel Internship with EduQuest

Started March 31, 2025

My journey with the Nobel Internship program began on March 31, 2025, marking a significant milestone in my coding adventure. Through EduQuest, I was introduced to a comprehensive learning environment that combined technical skills with leadership development.

The program focused on web design fundamentals, where I learned to create responsive and user-friendly interfaces. Working alongside experienced mentors and fellow interns, I gained valuable insights into the practical aspects of web development and project collaboration.

Web Design Class Facilitation

April 15, 2025

One of the highlights of my internship was facilitating an "Introduction to Web Design" class with my team. This experience not only reinforced my technical knowledge but also developed my communication and leadership skills. Teaching others helped me understand concepts more deeply and improved my ability to explain complex topics in simple terms.

Skills Developed

  • Technical Skills: HTML5, CSS3, responsive design principles
  • Leadership: Team collaboration, class facilitation, mentoring
  • Problem-solving: Debugging, troubleshooting, creative solutions
  • Communication: Presenting ideas, explaining concepts, teamwork
