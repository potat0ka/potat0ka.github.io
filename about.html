My Story

Who I am and what drives my passion for coding

Bigendra Shrestha

I'm a passionate software developer from Nepal, dedicated to solving real-world problems through innovative technology solutions. My coding journey began in March 2025 when I started the Nobel Internship program with EduQuest, where I discovered my love for web design, Python programming, and technical leadership.

Since then, I've immersed myself in continuous learning through various channels. I've explored Python fundamentals through Al Sweigart's "Automate the Boring Stuff with Python," enhanced my skills with DataCamp's comprehensive courses, and applied my knowledge in practical, real-world scenarios during my internship.

My ultimate goal is to become a versatile full-stack developer who can create meaningful applications that make a positive impact. I believe in the power of technology to transform lives and am committed to using my skills to inspire others and contribute to the global developer community.
